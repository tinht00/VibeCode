## Operating notes

- Seller-prompted AI art co the duoc ban neu van dap ung creativity standards va co human authorship/co-curation ro rang.
- Khong coi AI prompt bundle don thuan la mot loai hang hoa hop le de listing nhu mot thanh pham sang tao tren Etsy.
- Neu san pham su dung AI-generated imagery, description nen co disclosure ngan gon, ro rang.
- Listing title nen dua keyword chinh len dau khi hop ly, de doc, khong nhoi tat ca keyword vao title.
- Etsy cho phep toi da 13 tags; uu tien cum tu tu nhien, da dang, khong lap lai y nghia vo ich.
- Theo logic toi uu listing, moi tag nen nam trong gioi han 20 ky tu.
- Attributes va category giup search, khong can lap lai may moc trong tags neu da duoc cover.
- Listing media hien ho tro toi da 20 anh va 1 video; video ngan 5-15 giay la khuon huu dung cho listing.
- Alt text cho hinh anh co the bo sung de ho tro accessibility va external SEO.
- Digital item description can noi ro file type, so luong file, muc dich su dung, va khong co san pham vat ly duoc gui.

## Copy defaults

- Title: English only, keyword first, tranh adjective rong.
- Description: mo dau noi ro item, giua trinh bay what's included + use case + delivery, cuoi them disclosure neu can.
- Tags: 13 tags/set, natural phrases, khong duplicate nguyen van trong cung set.
