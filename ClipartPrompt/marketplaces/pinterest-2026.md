## Operating notes

- Organic/product distribution phu thuoc manh vao chat luong site, link, metadata va do tuoi tin cay cua merchant setup.
- Hosted catalog duoc ingest moi 24 gio; manual uploads can refresh trong 7 ngay de giu organic distribution, va 90 ngay de giu ads eligibility theo logic policy hien hanh.
- Verified Merchant can claimed domain, website du tuoi, data source/catalog du chat luong, va event signals du deu.
- Link quality rat quan trong; redirect khong minh bach, misleading destination, hoac link dang nghi co the bi chan phan phoi.
- Keyword nen xuat hien trong Pin title va Pin description mot cach tu nhien.
- Pinterest phu hop huong topic-led, use-case-led, seasonal-led hon la SKU stuffing.
- Pin copy khong nen hashtage-heavy; nen tap trung vao search intent va visual outcome.

## Copy defaults

- Tao 10 pin copy sets cho moi brief chinh.
- Moi set co title, description, board/topic keywords va angle rieng.
- Angle nen da dang: search intent, gifting, planning, aesthetic, productivity, seasonal, problem-solution, collection, tutorial-style, CTA nhe.
